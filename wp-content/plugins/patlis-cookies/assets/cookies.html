Necessary cookies help make a website usable by enabling basic functions such as page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

Marketing cookies are used to track visitors across websites. The intention is to display ads that are relevant and engaging for the individual user and therefore more valuable for publishers and third-party advertisers.

Preference cookies enable a website to remember information that changes the way the website behaves or looks, such as your preferred language or the region you are in.

Statistics cookies help website owners understand how visitors interact with websites by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

Unclassified cookies are cookies that we are currently in the process of classifying, together with the providers of individual cookies.
